  • About us

We are band musicians playing lots of different music styles and we are passionated about it. Thus we wanted to share our passion with you by opening our record store. We spent lots of time gathering all these treasures. This include formats such as CDs and 45 rpm records. Music wise, we love jazz, this is our first passion and what we learned to play. But we also love listening and playing rock, blues, soul, funk, reggae, disco and other styles as long as it is retro !

No we are not modern ! And yes we love being retro ! This is why we called our store The Retro Record Store. Pay us a visit and we promise you will enjoy the ride !
